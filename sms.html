About the project

The focus of this project was to give SecureMySocial's old website a complete modern and sleek look; the aim was to reorganize and present content concisely and attractively while effectively maintaining and conveying the SecureMySocial message.

Additionally, the client wanted to implement a blogging platform with total control of the content management system for their blog entries. This blog feature connected Blogger to the client’s website for live real-time updates.

OLD WEBSITE HOMEPAGE

WORK SAMPLES

After some research on other professional business website and determining the type of audience SecureMySocial would attract, I opted for an auto-play image carousel to display information for the three audience: businesses, parents, and individuals.

The client’s old website had a blog archive that redirected to separate individual pages — this was not a very efficient method of organizing and publishing content. To improve this process, integrate an existing content management system (CMS), such as Blogger, to the website would best serve as a blogging platform for the client to use.

This also meant that Blogger would have to be designed with the same look and feel as the website so visitors would not feel like they are having two separate experiences.

CHALLENGES AND SOLUTIONS

The freedom of redesigning this website was what made it a fun yet challenging task. To start, I knew that most websites opt for a long, single page scroll layout with call to actions leading to other heavy contents.

The other main issue that I wanted to address was how the information was organized and displayed. Also, since the client wanted to keep their logo, the new website had to have a similar color scheme and feel based on the logo’s colors.

FINAL PRODUCT

The final project deliverables included:

• Complete site redesign

• Homepage restructuring which included a video section

• Blogger integration

• Mobile responsiveness
