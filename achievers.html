Website Redesign:

The Story

Worked closely with content and design members to restructure the old corporate website to a more sleek and modern design, with a focus on clear content messaging and brand voice. Also, utilizing timed animation with TweenMax and TweenLite to add some life and movement to images on a still page to help convey a message.

First section of the homepage ft. new menu bar

Challenges

The main challenge was to develop based off mock-ups received from designers whose designs often broke the grid for responsive development. In the end, multiple solutions were found based on each individual situations.

Another challenge was learning different libraries to attain the desired animation. In this case, other than TweenMax and TweenLite, ProgressBar.js was used to animate the statistics to make it less flat on the page.

Tools Used

• HTML • CSS/LESS • JavaScript • Drupal • TweenLite/TweenMax • Adobe Photoshop • Adobe Illustrator
