About the Project

I created recruitment banners for Northwestern Polytechnic University as a side project from my regular website design and development responsibilities. NPU has two schools, School of Business and School of Engineering, that needed more advertising at university recruitment fairs. The banners were easily identifiable among the crowd and drew attention to NPU's table for school administrators to speak to prospective students.
