Corporate Event 2016:

Overview

Achievers Customer Experience (ACE) is an annual two-day user conference with over 500 attendees. At the event, Achievers brings together current and prospective clients, renowned guest speakers, and industry analysts, to share best practices for employee success and engagement in the modern workplace and announce new product releases.

The Work

I was responsible for working on the Agenda page to show the times of each session along with its details and speaker information. Most importantly, I needed to make sure that this page accurately displayed the time blocks for all desktops, tablets, and phones, as most people would more likely be on their mobile devices.

One of the challenges that faced was the time block in 15 minute increments which required a little more arithmetic configurations and CSS manipulations to get them displayed correctly across all platforms.
