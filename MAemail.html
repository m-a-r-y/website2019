About the Project

One of the projects for Mobile Action involved creating an HTML template for daily emails. These emails contain data snapshots for the users to be informed on their app performances without having to check the dashboard.

The Old Email Layout

The first step was to study the old layout and identify what areas could be improved in the new email layout. The old email failed to show relevant summaries for the users, therefore, the new email aim to correct the issue.

Creating Wireframes & High-Fidelity Prototypes

The old emails contained limited information that ran extra long so the redesign focused on creating space efficiency for relevant information. I began with building the main structures for the email HTML, coding the basic columns and inserted temporary dummy data as placeholders.

Wireframing the structure

Prototyping the first version with HTML/CSS

Final Product

In the end, the new layout satisfed the requirements for displaying quick summaries of the user's data across most email platforms. This new layout helped the user stay engaged with Mobile Action's product for monitoring their app's performances in the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.
